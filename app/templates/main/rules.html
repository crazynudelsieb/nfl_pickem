üéØ Core Rules

One Pick Per Week

Each game week, you select ONE team that you believe will win their game. Choose wisely - this is your only pick for that week!

No Repeat Teams

During the regular season (Weeks 1-18), you cannot pick the same team twice. Teams reset for playoffs, so choose strategically!

No Consecutive Losers

If your pick loses, you must wait at least one game week before you can pick that team again. This prevents immediate re-picks after a loss.

üèÜ Scoring System

‚úÖ Correct Pick

  • +1 point for the win
  • +Margin of victory as tiebreaker points
  • Example: Team wins by 14? You get +1 win, +14 tiebreaker

‚ùå Incorrect Pick

  • 0 points - no win credit
  • -Margin of loss as tiebreaker penalty
  • Example: Team loses by 7? You get 0 wins, -7 tiebreaker
How Tiebreakers Work:

Tiebreaker points only matter when two or more players have the same number of wins. The player with the higher tiebreaker total ranks higher. This rewards picking teams that win decisively and penalizes picking teams that lose badly.

üèÖ Playoff Qualification

Who Makes the Playoffs?

After the regular season (18 weeks), the top 4 players advance to the playoffs. Rankings are determined by:

  1. Total wins (most important)
  2. Tiebreaker points (if wins are equal)

Playoff Changes

  • Teams reset: All teams become available again, even if you used them in regular season
  • Same scoring: Still 1 point per win + margin as tiebreaker
  • Fewer games: Only playoff teams are playing, making choices more strategic

üí° Strategy Tips

üéØ Save Strong Teams

Don't use all the best teams early. Save powerhouse teams for difficult weeks when other options look weak.

üìÖ Plan Ahead

Look at the upcoming schedule. If a great team has a tough matchup this week but an easy one next week, consider waiting.

When tied in wins, tiebreaker points decide rankings. Picking teams in blowouts helps, but be careful - big losses hurt your tiebreaker!

üö´ Avoid Bad Streaks

Remember the "no consecutive losers" rule. If you pick a team and they lose, you can't use them next week. Plan around this!
