About NFL Pick'em

The ultimate NFL prediction game for friends, family, and coworkers.

Simple & Fun

Pick the team you think will win each week. No complicated point spreads or over/unders - just straight wins and losses.

Compete with Friends

Create private groups with your friends, family, or colleagues. See who really knows football best!

Real-Time Scoring

Scores and standings update automatically as games finish. Watch your position change throughout the week!

Season-Long Fun

Play all season long from Week 1 through the playoffs. Every pick matters in the race for the championship!

How It Works

1

Make Your Picks

Each week, pick one team that you think will win. You can only pick each team once per season.

2

Watch & Wait

Games play out during the week. If your team wins, you advance. If they lose, you're eliminated.

3

Survive to Win

The last player standing wins! If multiple players remain, tiebreakers determine the champion.
